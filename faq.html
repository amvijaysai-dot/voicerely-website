PRICING & COST

Is there a setup fee?
Yes, a one-time onboarding fee applies based on your requirements — number of CRM integrations, custom call flows, and voice training. We discuss this during your trial setup so there are no surprises.
What happens if I exceed my minutes?
Base plan charges $0.25/min, Starter charges $0.20/min. Growth and Enterprise include unlimited usage. We'll notify you before you reach your limits.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Upgrade immediately anytime. Downgrades apply on your next billing cycle.

HOW IT WORKS

How does the AI sound human?
We use advanced voice AI with natural language understanding. It adapts tone, pacing and conversation style dynamically, making conversations feel natural and human.
What happens if the AI cannot answer?
It instantly escalates using whichever method you configure: • Human transfer • SMS notification • Callback scheduling
How long does setup take?
Typical onboarding takes 5–7 business days including voice configuration, CRM integration, workflow setup and testing.

INTEGRATIONS

Which CRMs are supported?
HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, Zoho, Calendly, Pipedrive, and 20+ more via API and Zapier.
Do I need to change my phone number?
No. Simply forward your existing number to Voicerely or let us provision a dedicated number.

SECURITY

What happens if the system goes down?
Growth and Enterprise include 99.9% uptime with automatic failover to your backup destination.
Is my data secure?
Enterprise includes SOC 2, GDPR and HIPAA compliance. Calls remain encrypted and are never used for third-party AI training.
