Questions, answered

Is there a setup fee?
Yes, a one-time setup fee applies to every plan. It's not a fixed number — it depends entirely on the type of services and integrations you need (number of agents, CRM connections, custom scripts, etc.). We'll walk through your specific setup and give you an exact figure during your trial setup call.
What happens if I exceed my minutes?
On Base and Starter, extra minutes are billed at your plan's overage rate — no call is ever dropped or blocked. Growth and Enterprise plans include enough volume that overage typically doesn't apply.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Yes, anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle, and we'll help you pick the right tier as your call volume changes.
What's included in onboarding?
We build and train your voice agent on your scripts, FAQs, and booking flow, connect it to your CRM and calendar, run live test calls with you, and stay hands-on until it's answering exactly the way you want.
Is my data secure, and are you HIPAA-friendly?
Call data is encrypted in transit and at rest. For medical and legal clients with compliance requirements, we can discuss HIPAA-aligned handling and data agreements during onboarding.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no long-term lock-in — cancel anytime and you won't be billed for the next cycle.
Do you offer a guarantee?
We stand behind onboarding until your agent is handling calls the way you expect. Reach out to your account contact for specifics on your plan.
